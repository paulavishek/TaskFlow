Solving the Kanban Column Height Problem

The Problem You Described

When a Kanban column becomes much longer than other columns (like your "Backlog" column), it becomes nearly impossible to drag tasks from the bottom of the long column to shorter columns (like "Review") because:

  • There's no drop zone available at the same vertical level
  • You can't scroll while holding a dragged task
  • The shorter columns don't provide enough space to drop tasks

Auto-Scroll Feature

When dragging tasks, the page automatically scrolls if you move your mouse near the top or bottom edges. Visual indicators show scroll direction.

Dynamic Drop Zones

Short columns temporarily expand during drag operations, providing ample space to drop tasks from long columns.

Keyboard Navigation

Press Tab to focus tasks, then use arrow keys to move between columns. Perfect for accessibility and power users.

Mobile Optimized

Enhanced touch targets and responsive design ensure smooth drag-and-drop on mobile devices.

Key Improvements Summary

These enhancements solve your exact problem: you can now easily move tasks from the bottom of long columns to any other column, regardless of height differences. The interface provides multiple ways to accomplish this task, from improved drag-and-drop to keyboard shortcuts.
